Our Mission

Medicine centered on people, not paperwork.

Audit Trail Health was founded on a simple belief: every patient deserves unhurried, expert attention from a physician who knows them. We have built a network of specialists, primary care doctors, and support staff who share that belief — and the systems to back it up.

From a routine check-up to a complex referral pathway, our integrated records and scheduling ensure nothing falls through the cracks. Your health history, billing, and care team are all in one place.

Patient Experience

From registration to recovery

01

Create Your Account

Register online in minutes. We collect your basic information, insurance details, and assign you a primary care physician from our network.

02

Book an Appointment

Log into your patient portal to schedule visits with your primary physician or any specialist. View availability across all seven of our locations.

03

Receive Care

Your physician documents your diagnosis and treatment plan directly in our system. Need a specialist? Referrals are issued and tracked digitally.

04

Billing & Follow-up

After your visit, billing is generated automatically and your treatment history is updated. Review invoices and payment status from your portal at any time.
